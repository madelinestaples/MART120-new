My Story

I am currently a sophmore in college. I became interested in media arts when I started doing yearbook in highschool. I fell in love with photography, design and technology. I started doing photography on the side along with school and work and began to wonder where else I could take this. I decided to go to the University of Montana because I was really excited to be in their media arts program. I am so happy that I am in this program, and I feel so lucky to take classes where I really love the material. I am so excited to see where this major takes me!
